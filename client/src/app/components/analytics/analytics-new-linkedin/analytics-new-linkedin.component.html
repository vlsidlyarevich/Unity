Github Analysis tool

Analyze person's github profile and you will know about his favourite languages and technologies he prefers.

Repositories Overview

Overview user repositories, look for most popular, most starred or most huge repositories. There is also a possibility to navigate to repo's Github page.

Technologies usage

Explore technologies and languages that user use. Have a look at their percentage diagrams, to understand user's experience in different fields. Also look at technologies usage over time.

Commits and collaborations

Browse user's activity at Github, his commits and collaborations. Maybe he is working on Well-known open source projects? Or maybe he has his own?
